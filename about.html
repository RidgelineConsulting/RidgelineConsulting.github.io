About

Built by someone who's done the work.

I've spent 13 years in defense contracting. Estimating, project management, and operations for companies that build things the Navy depends on. In that world, the job isn't reading the spec. It's reading the room. Figuring out what's actually going on behind what someone wrote down or what they told you in a meeting.

That's what I do now for small and mid-size companies. I walk in, ask questions until I find the real problem, not the symptom someone called me about, and build the fix. Sometimes that's a workforce structure that doesn't exist yet. Sometimes it's a lead generation system that runs while you sleep. Sometimes it's just showing you that the process you've been routing around for five years can be replaced in a week.

I use AI heavily in how I deliver. It lets me move at a speed and price point that a traditional consulting firm can't touch. But the tool isn't the point. The point is knowing which questions to ask and which silence in the room to pay attention to.

Based in Wabash, Indiana. Working with companies across northern Indiana who know something needs to change but aren't sure where to start.

My Approach

Diagnostic conversation.

Every engagement starts the same way. I research your company, ask where it hurts, identify who owns the problem, and watch for the "umms in the room." Then I trace what's broken back to the root cause and build from there.

1

2

Build
