Proof of Work

01

Workforce Architecture & Career Framework

Industrial Contractor · 40+ Field Employees
+

The Problem

The entire technical workforce was classified under one generic title. A senior specialist and an entry-level hire held the same title. No visibility into capabilities, no career progression, no formal job descriptions.

What We Built

A complete career framework with 10+ labor categories and 30+ unique role/level combinations. Standardized job descriptions, updated org chart, full employee mapping with compensation modeling for cost-neutral rollout.

The Result

Every employee received a title reflecting actual capabilities. Leadership gained real workforce visibility and a defensible progression structure. Zero unplanned compensation impact.

02

Estimation Department Build-Out

Industrial Contractor
+

The Problem

No formalized estimation process. Bids built ad hoc without standardized tools. Pricing was inconsistent and hard to defend. Money left on the table or costs eaten without warning.

What We Built

A complete estimation function: tools, labor frameworks, pricing models, and workflows connecting estimation to project management and field operations. Built for daily production use.

The Result

200+ production estimates completed using the system. Standardized, repeatable, and audit-ready. The team can defend every number.
