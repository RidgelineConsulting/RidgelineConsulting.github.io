Services

Every engagement starts the same way.

We ask questions until we find what's actually broken. Then we build the fix. What that fix looks like depends entirely on what we find. Here's what it usually turns into.

How We Work

Diagnosis before prescription.

Most consulting firms show up with a solution looking for a problem. We start with the problem. The real one, not the symptom you called about.

We research your company, talk to the people doing the work, trace what's broken back to the root cause, and map the gap between where you are and where you need to be. That diagnostic shapes everything that comes next.

Sometimes the fix takes a week. Sometimes it's a 90-day build. Sometimes it's ongoing support. But it always starts with understanding what's actually going on before we touch anything.

What a Diagnostic Produces

Issue inventory ranked by cost, risk, and urgency
Root-cause analysis with evidence, not assumptions
KPI baseline showing where you are today
Execution plan with owners, timelines, and acceptance criteria
Clear stop point: you decide what happens next
Core Service

Workforce Architecture & Career Frameworks

Your people don't see a future at your company. Not because there isn't one, but because you've never defined it. A senior specialist and a first-year hire carry the same title. There's no progression, no structure, and no way for anyone to know where they stand or where they're headed.

That's how you lose your best foreman to the shop down the road offering a title and a raise you would've matched if you'd seen it coming.

We build the career infrastructure from the ground up. Labor categories, title progressions, job descriptions written for your actual operation, compensation frameworks that make sense, and a retention structure that gives people a reason to stay.

Timeline 2-6 weeks depending on org complexity
Deliverables Role catalog, leveling framework, career paths, job descriptions, compensation structure

Signs You Need This

Everyone on the crew has the same job title regardless of skill or experience
You can't explain to a new hire what their growth path looks like
You've lost people to competitors offering clearer structure
Job descriptions are pulled off the internet or don't exist
Compensation decisions are made ad hoc with no framework to back them up
Core Service

Process Documentation & Operational Systems

Your company runs on tribal knowledge. The people who've been here longest know how things work, and everyone else is guessing. When someone leaves, the knowledge walks out the door with them.

We document what your best people already do, build it into repeatable systems, and create the operating cadences that keep things on track. SOPs that people actually use, training materials that reduce ramp-up time, and scorecards that tell you what's working without asking.

Timeline 1-4 weeks depending on scope
Deliverables SOP suite, RACI matrix, operating cadence, KPI scoreboard, training documentation

Signs You Need This

New hires take months to get productive
Quality varies depending on who's working that day
You've been "meaning to write things down" for years
The same mistakes keep happening and nobody can explain why
An audit or customer review is coming and you're not ready
Core Service

Targeted Lead Generation

You need more work in the pipeline, but you don't have a business development person and you don't have time to chase it yourself. So the pipeline stays thin, and growth depends on word-of-mouth and repeat business.

We build targeted prospect lists segmented by your actual service lines, with named contacts, trigger signals, and outreach angles. Not a database dump. Research-grade targeting delivered on a cadence your sales team can work.

Model Monthly subscription with weekly deliverables
Deliverables Segmented prospect lists, named contacts, trigger signals, messaging angles, outreach plan

What You Get

Prospect lists segmented by your service lines and target verticals
Named decision-makers with contact information
"Why them" notes: expansion signals, hiring spikes, contract awards, new facilities
Messaging angles tailored to each segment
Weekly outreach plan your team can execute immediately
Industry Service

Estimation & Project Systems

Your estimates are inconsistent. They depend on whoever happens to run the numbers. When bids come in wrong, nobody can explain the variance because there's no standardized method, no historical tracking, and no review process.

Everyone knows the system is broken. Nobody fixes it because fixing it means slowing down, and slowing down means you're not bidding, not winning, not producing. So the broken process stays and people work around it.

We build estimation systems that improve accuracy without grinding your operation to a halt. Standardized methodology, templates, historical data integration, variance tracking, and review cadences that layer into your existing workflow instead of replacing it.

Timeline 2-4 weeks for system build, parallel to live operations
Deliverables Estimation methodology, templates, historical data framework, variance tracking system, review cadence

Signs You Need This

Estimate accuracy depends on which person runs the numbers
You can't explain variance when bids come in wrong
There's no historical data tracking to improve future estimates
New estimators take months to ramp because nothing is documented
You've wanted to fix this for years but can't afford to stop bidding
