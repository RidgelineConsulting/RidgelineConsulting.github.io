Core Service

Workforce Architecture & Career Frameworks

Your people don't see a future at your company. Not because there isn't one, but because you've never defined it. A senior specialist and a first-year hire carry the same title. There's no progression, no structure, and no way for anyone to know where they stand or where they're headed.

That's how you lose your best foreman to the shop down the road offering a title and a raise you would've matched if you'd seen it coming.

I build the career infrastructure from the ground up. Labor categories, title progressions, job descriptions written for your actual operation, compensation modeling that lets you roll it out without blowing up your payroll. Your team gets clarity. You get visibility into your workforce and a defensible structure that holds up when you need it to.

Everything I build is ready for your team to use on day one. Not a binder. Not a set of slides. Working frameworks with real titles, real expectations, and real progression paths that keep your people where they belong.

Timeline 6 to 10 weeks, depending on headcount and complexity
Best For Companies with 20+ employees that have outgrown their informal structure
Deliverables Career framework, job descriptions, org chart, employee mapping, compensation model

Signs You Need This

Everyone on the crew has the same job title regardless of skill or experience
You can't explain to a new hire what their growth path looks like
You've lost people to competitors offering clearer structure
Job descriptions are pulled off the internet or don't exist
Compensation decisions are made ad hoc with no framework to back them up
