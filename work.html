01

Workforce Architecture & Career Framework

Industrial Contractor · 40+ Field Employees
+

The Problem

The entire technical workforce was classified under one generic title. A senior specialist with 15 years on the tools and a week-one hire held the same role. No visibility into capabilities, no career progression, no formal job descriptions. Leadership couldn't explain the org to themselves, let alone to a new hire or auditor.

What I Built

A complete career framework with 10+ labor categories and 30+ unique role/level combinations. Standardized job descriptions for every position. Updated org chart. Full employee mapping with compensation modeling designed for cost-neutral rollout.

The Result

Every employee received a title that reflected their actual capabilities. Leadership gained real workforce visibility and a defensible progression structure. Zero unplanned compensation impact. The framework is still in daily use.

02

Estimation Department Build-Out

Industrial Contractor
+

The Problem

No formalized estimation process. Bids were built ad hoc without standardized tools. Pricing was inconsistent and hard to defend. Money was being left on the table or costs eaten without warning. Every estimate was a one-off.

What I Built

A complete estimation function from scratch: tools, labor frameworks, pricing models, and workflows connecting estimation to project management and field operations. Built for daily production use, not a shelf.

The Result

200+ production estimates completed using the system. Standardized, repeatable, and audit-ready. The team can defend every number. The system is still in active use years later.

03

Targeted Lead Generation System

Regional Transportation Company
+

The Problem

The company needed new business but didn't have the staff or budget for dedicated business development. The owner knew growth was out there but didn't have the time or tools to chase it. Pipeline depended on whoever happened to call in.

What I Built

A targeted prospecting system that identifies qualified leads, compiles contact information, and delivers a professional report with actionable targets segmented by service type. From initial brief to finished report: under 20 minutes.

The Result

A lead report that would have taken a junior BD analyst days to compile, delivered in minutes. The company's new sales hire is using the reports as their first working tool. New revenue conversations started within the week.
