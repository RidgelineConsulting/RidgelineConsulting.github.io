Every engagement starts the same way: someone knows something is broken but isn't sure exactly what. Here's what we found and what we built.

01

Workforce Architecture for a Growing Industrial Operation

40+ employees, manufacturing & field services
+

The Problem

A senior specialist and a first-year hire had the same title. There were no career paths, no leveling structure, and no framework to explain compensation decisions. Retention was suffering and the owner couldn't articulate why people were leaving.

What We Built

Complete role catalog covering 30+ role/level combinations. Title progressions, job descriptions written for the actual operation, compensation bands, and a career framework the team could see and understand.

The Result

The company could now explain to every employee where they stood, what the next step looked like, and what it would take to get there. Compensation decisions became defensible. Retention conversations changed.

02

Estimation System Overhaul

Defense contractor, 200+ production estimates
+

The Problem

Estimates were inconsistent, undocumented, and dependent on whoever happened to run the numbers. There was no standardized process, no historical tracking, and no way to explain variance when bids came in wrong.

What We Built

Standardized estimation methodology with templates, historical data integration, variance tracking, and a review cadence. The process went from individual guesswork to a repeatable system with audit trail.

The Result

200+ production estimates processed through the new system. Variance tracking enabled continuous improvement. New estimators could ramp up in weeks instead of months. The department could defend its numbers.

03

Targeted Lead Generation for a Transportation Company

Trucking & freight, multi-segment operation
+

The Problem

The company needed a bigger pipeline but had no dedicated business development staff. Growth depended on existing relationships and word-of-mouth. The owner knew the segments but didn't have time to hunt for prospects.

What We Built

Segmented prospect reports covering reefer, flatbed, dry van, LTL, and specialty services. Named contacts with company details, trigger signals, and outreach angles tailored to each segment. Delivered in under 24 hours.

The Result

The company's new sales hire received a research-grade target list on day one. Instead of cold-starting from scratch, they had qualified prospects with context and a plan for reaching them.
